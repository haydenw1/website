Education

I am currently a 4th year student at the Rochester Institute of Technology (RIT) in the Media Arts and Technology program (Bachelor of Science).

The Media Arts and Technology major at RIT is focused on teaching students how to work with Design, Digital Media, and Print.

DESIGN

  • Traditonal and Digital Magazine Layout
  • Photo Editing and Retouching

DIGITAL MEDIA

  • Cross Media Techniques and Platforms (dynamic printing)

PRINT

  • Traditional Print Techniques and Equipment

My experiences and preferences have pushed me towards the technical elements offered by this major; specifically web development, user interaction and interface design, dynamic email solutions, and database usage and management.

Throughout all my work I try to provide users or viewers with a consistent interaction and design scheme, accompanied by great page layout and typography.

Activities

SILVER WINGS

Silver Wings is a national co-ed professional organization. It is made up of civilians dedicated to developing our members personally and professionally, volunteering in our communities, and educating others about national defense.

Silver Wings is associated with the U.S. Air Force and several other military organizations. Some of our members are Air Force Reserve Officers' Training Corps (AFROTC) cadets acting in a civilian capacity; however, I am not a cadet (just an interested civilian).

My time in Silver Wings has taught me the importance of communication and the difficulties that come with undertaking new projects and initiatives, particularly across long distances. While in Silver Wings I have also been given opportunities to speak to groups ranging from 20 to 200 people.
