Submitting a Job to the DPC

Uploading to SPM-Portal (short)

This is quick guide to using our new system, SPM-Portal. For the detailed version, please reference the full-sheet document, Uploading to SPM-Portal (long).

  1. Go to SPM-Portal, spm-portal.rit.edu.

  2. Select RIT Login and then use your DCE user to login.

  3. Select the DPC Dropbox volume. Remember the flilowing:

    1. Files must be PDF (or TIFF for wide format prints).

    2. Use only letters, numbers, and under scores in file names.

    3. Combine PDFs that have the same printing specs into one PDF.

  4. Click Upload New Files, browse your file(s), and hit Upload Files.

  5. Once files have finished uploading, click Done.

  6. When your file(s) show on the main page, click Edit Job Ticket.

  7. Fill out all the information listed on the page.

  8. Click Apply Changes, and wait for the page to reload.

  9. Click Submit Job.*

* For us to print your job, you must come down to the DPC.
